YongJun Lee

Chef of a small tteokbok shop in South Korea.

Story about the food

Since he was a child, when he would come home from school looking depressed, his mother would often make Tteokbokki for him. Believing that eating spicy food relieves stress, she would often make Tteokbokki for her son. The two of them would eat it, crying and laughing at the spiciness of the food, and eventually feel better. Even as an adult, on days when he was feeling down, he would often make and eat Tteokbokki, remembering the precious moments when he and his mom laughed together. Tteokbokki has always been a source of comfort for him.
