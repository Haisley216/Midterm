Family's celebration, sukiyaki

Chef

Kangrok Choi

A Korean chef who specializes in Japanese cuisine. He became famous as the winner of the cooking show 'Master Chef Korea'.

Story about the food

Although his parents were strongly against him becoming a chef, he harbored a desire to cook and practiced constantly. After many years of hard work, his parents finally approved of his dreams after eating sukiyaki, the food he was most confident in.After that, he opened his own Japanese restaurant and won a popular Korean cooking program. Sukiyaki meant so much to him when his parents first allowed him to cook, that even after he became a celebrity chef, he never missed a celebration without sukiyaki.
